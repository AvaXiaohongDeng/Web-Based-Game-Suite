    How to play
  • Press the button "Roll the dice" and the computer shows the total. After 5 turns, it presents your winns and loses.
  • First turn
  • if the total is 2,3 or 12, the players loses.
  • if the total is 7 or 11, the players wins.
  • if the total is 4,5,6,8,9 or 10, that number becomes the "point".
  • Roll again! the total is same as the point, the player wins, otherwise loses.
  • Next turn
  • Game over after 5 turns
